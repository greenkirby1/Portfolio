Something About Me

The prospect of gaining insight into meticulous systems and creating anew have always ignited a longstanding passion within me. Whether it is pouring out lines of code, reseraching in a biomedical lab or putting strokes onto a canvas; they all require the utmost dedication and honing of basic skillsets, to see the results come to fruition and see the light of day. Even with the frustration and struggles along the way, the journey inbetween is what excites me the most.

I relish in combining the scientific and logical methods with the creative and out-of-box thinking, when it comes to solving problems.
